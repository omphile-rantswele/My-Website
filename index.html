About Us

He has sent me to proclaim that the time has come When the LORD will save his people and defeat their enemies. He has sent me to comfort all who mourn, to give to those who mourn in Zion joy and gladness instead of grief, a song of praise instead of sorrow. Our Services

Ministry

We endeavour to reach the lost at all cost and preach in remote rural areas where there are no churches. With the help of financial and prayer partners, we plant churches to establish and engraft Godâ€™s people in the Body of Christ through a simple message of the cross. We train leaders who continue with the work to reach more souls in their respective communities. Outside and within the parameters of the Church, I strive and aim at improving our communities by organizing programs that empowers and benefit people from all walks of life.

Vision

We endeavour to raise a generation of Champions, Winners and Overcomers in life through a practical, active and living faith; to help ordinary people live a lifestyle of faith (2 Corinthians 5:7). Anything which comes from God is able to overcome the world: and the power by which we have overcome the world is our faith. (1John 5:4)

Mission

Our mission is to Proclaim the liberating Word of God to every creature And Make Disciples of every nation, tribe and language by Godly Instruction (Teaching) and Leadership with Love. Appreciating the work of the cross by Restoring every man to the Kingdom and Strengthen the Believers. To plant churches and preach CHRIST where He has not been preached.

Feeding & Clothing

Through the help of our generous friends and partners, we have feeding programs that aim at alleviating the plight of widows and orphans who are mainly children, youth and women. We have a goal of reaching the unreached (especially in remote rural villages) with the liberating and restoring Gospel of Jesus Christ. As God provides, with the little resources we have; we still do outreaches and plant churches in those remote villages.

Aims And Objectives

  • To promulgate the Gospel of Jesus Christ by all scriptural means to the world in order to bring Salvation to all mankind, recognizing the Word of God as our all-sufficient rule and guide.

  • To teach and preach the Word of God with clarity and simplicity.

  • To regularly administer the ordinances of Jesus Christ, namely Holy Communion and water baptism.

  • To encourage Believers to operate in the gifts of the Holy Spirit.

  • To plan and organize social projects those are aimed at bringing social justice and [emotional, spiritual, economic and physical] relief to the victims of oppression.

  • To train members of the faith community so that they may fulfil their function in the Body of Christ.

  • To establish ministries aimed at giving Christian instructions and education to children, youth and adults.

  • To care for widows, orphans, Aids victims, alcohol and drug addicts victims, the aged and the needy in a practical way.

  • To encourage the recognition of all true Believers as members of the Body of Christ and promote unity and fellowship amongst them.
